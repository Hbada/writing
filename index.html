HB Copywriting

by Heidi Bada

The recipe for quick and great results! I will assess your goals and ideas, to craft ideal copy for you. For every job, I draw techniques from over ten years of news, business, and technical writing experience, and choose the right methods for your project. I worry about the details, so you can get back to business.

Then I write so you don't have to!

P.S. Complex information is welcome here; I love to sort out information and make it clear for the chosen audience.

"I love tackling dense information and collections of data that need to be reorganized and clarified for impact."

- writer Heidi Bada

My writing experience includes: speeches, sales letters, websites (sports, military, arts), hospital brochures, newspapers and newsletters, podcast scripts, PowerPoint sales presentations, health apps, childrens' educational apps, marketing and business plans, and nonprofit health and arts policy.
