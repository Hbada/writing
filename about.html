About this copywriter

Raised and trained in Canada initially as a data collection and technical communications specialist, followed by accounting and sociology studies, Heidi Bada is a skilled communicator who works with complex information that needs to be adjusted and tailored to consumers, business, and scientific/technical audiences.

Her training was initially scientific writing, and has grown to include writing for websites and marketing documents in demanding fields such as health and sports.


Project highlights

  • Department of National Defence, Canada - wrote and published new program website full of health info
  • Island Health, BC - print materials copy editing and website user experience assessment, with 'patient-centred' approach
  • AquaVic Water Solutions Inc. - edited 200-page report for environmental engineering company
  • Wikipedia - edited outdated art history pages on request of local art teacher
  • Camosun College, BC - college teams' sports photography
  • Department of National Defence, Canada - mental health articles for military newspaper
  • Camosun College, BC - news articles in science and sports for college campus newspaper
  • YMCA, Victoria, BC - human resources retention factors report
